It all started with a simple question: “Maa, how much does this store make in a day?” That childhood curiosity led me to a lifelong passion for business. Instead of following the traditional engineering route, I pursued Business and later earned my MBA, leading me to roles in Brand Management (ZEE Entertainment), Healthcare (Baxter), and B2B E-commerce (Schneider Electric). Each chapter sharpened my expertise—marketing, business strategy, global operations. But the real breakthrough? Seeing firsthand how supply chain disruptions impact industries worldwide. That’s why I co-founded Maketronics. Our mission is clear: to solve the global challenge of component obsolescence. We empower industries like Automotive, IoT, Med-Tech, Aviation, and Defence to source critical components, minimize risks, and keep innovating—without limits.
